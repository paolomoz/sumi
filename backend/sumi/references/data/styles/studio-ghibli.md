# studio-ghibli

Soft, luminous hand-painted animation aesthetic inspired by Studio Ghibli and Hayao Miyazaki — watercolor washes of gentle sky blues and warm sunset oranges, impossibly lush green landscapes with individual grass blades catching light, cumulus clouds piled like cotton mountains against gradient skies. Every frame feels like a pastoral daydream painted with gouache and devotion, where nature is alive and magical creatures might peek from behind any tree.

## Color Palette

- Primary: Sky blue (#87CEEB), grass green (#7CB342), warm cream (#FFF8E7), sunset orange (#FF9E80)
- Background: Soft gradient skies transitioning from pale blue (#B4D7E8) at horizon to deeper blue (#5DADE2) above, or warm golden hour amber (#FFCC80) to dusky purple (#9575CD)
- Accents: Soft pink (#F8BBD9), moss green (#8BC34A), terracotta roof (#D84315), deep forest (#2E7D32), dusty purple (#9C89B8)
- Text: Warm dark brown (#4E342E) or soft charcoal (#455A64), never harsh black

## Visual Elements

- Soft watercolor-style washes with visible color gradations and gentle wet-edge bleeding
- Layered cumulus clouds rendered as voluminous sculptural forms with subtle purple shadows and cream highlights
- Grass depicted as thousands of individual blades catching light, not flat green fields — visible texture at medium distance
- Trees with detailed individual leaf clusters creating rhythmic organic shapes against sky
- Warm golden-hour or blue-hour lighting casting long soft shadows
- Architecture with visible weathering: moss on roof tiles, rust stains on metal, peeling paint
- Small background details that reward close inspection: a butterfly on a flower, a frog on a lily pad, laundry drying on a line
- Skies rendered as smooth gradients from horizon to zenith, never flat single-color
- Dust motes and pollen floating in shafts of light
- Steam, mist, and atmospheric haze softening distant elements
- Windows glowing warm orange from interior light against twilight exteriors
- Rounded, soft edges on mechanical objects — no harsh industrial aesthetic
- Small creatures (insects, birds, forest spirits) adding life to scenes

## Compositional Patterns

| Content Structure | Composition | Reference |
|-------------------|-------------|-----------|
| Hierarchy/levels | Layered landscape with foreground flowers, midground characters, background mountains receding into atmospheric haze | Nausicaa's valley — depth through atmospheric perspective |
| Flow/process | Winding path through landscape, each bend revealing a new scene or element | Country road from Totoro — journey through the countryside |
| Connections | Bridges, trains, or flying machines linking floating islands or distant locations | Castle in the Sky — connecting impossible spaces |
| Cycles | Seasonal progression through same landscape — cherry blossoms to summer green to autumn gold | The passage of time shown through nature's changes |
| Comparison | Split scene: cozy interior warmth on one side, vast exterior nature on the other | Looking out a window at an expansive landscape |
| Categories | Village with distinct buildings each representing a category — bakery, workshop, home | Kiki's seaside town — each building with personality |
| Growth | Small seedling in foreground, mature tree in background, ancient giant on horizon | Forest at different stages of life, passage of time |
| Networks | Overhead view of village paths connecting homes, or root systems beneath a forest | Interconnected community shown through physical paths |
| Overview | High aerial view over patchwork farmland, forests, and villages receding to misty horizon | Flying sequence looking down at the miniaturized world |
| Journey | Linear progression from left (departure) through middle (adventure) to right (destination) | Train sequence from Spirited Away — movement through space |

## Typography

- Soft, rounded sans-serif fonts with gentle weight — no harsh geometric angles
- Hand-lettered appearance preferred for titles, as if painted with a brush
- Text integrated into scene as signs, banners, or book titles within the illustration
- Warm brown or soft charcoal text color, never pure black
- Generous letter-spacing, readable and calm
- Titles can have subtle watercolor texture or soft drop shadow
- Labels should feel like handwritten annotations, not clinical captions
- Japanese text elements (if appropriate) in traditional brush calligraphy style

## Anti-Patterns

- **NOT anime generic** — Studio Ghibli has a distinctive soft, painterly quality completely different from sharp-lined modern anime. No hard cel-shading, no stark black outlines, no flat color fills.
- **NOT Disney/Pixar 3D** — Ghibli is hand-painted 2D watercolor aesthetic. No glossy plastic surfaces, no subsurface scattering, no 3D modeling artifacts.
- **NOT dark fantasy** — Even in dramatic moments, Ghibli maintains warmth and hope. No grimdark palettes, no excessive desaturation, no horror aesthetics.
- **NOT minimalist** — Ghibli backgrounds are richly detailed with thousands of small touches. Empty space should be atmospheric (sky, mist), not stark negative space.
- **NOT photorealistic** — This is illustration, not photography. Colors are heightened, edges are painterly, light is idealized golden-hour even when it shouldn't be.
- **NOT sharp and clinical** — Everything should feel soft, warm, and inviting. No hard edges, no sterile whites, no machine precision.
- **NOT busy with text** — Let the illustration breathe. Information should emerge from the imagery, with minimal text overlays.
- This must look like a background painting from a Ghibli film — lush, detailed, atmospheric, painted with visible brushwork and luminous color gradients — not a generic "anime-style" illustration.
