# art-nouveau

Flowing organic lines, natural forms, and elegant ornamentation inspired by the Art Nouveau movement (1890-1910). Also known as Jugendstil, Stile Liberty, Modernisme.

## Color Palette

- Primary: Sage green, dusty rose, peacock blue (deep teal-blue), burgundy
- Background: Ivory/cream warm whites, soft gradient backdrops
- Accents: Gold/bronze metallic, copper, olive earth tones

### Palette Combinations

| Combination | Colors |
|-------------|--------|
| Classic | Sage green + dusty rose + gold + ivory |
| Rich | Peacock blue + burgundy + gold + cream |
| Natural | Olive + copper + ivory + sage |
| Elegant | Gold + black + ivory + dusty rose |

## Variants

| Variant | Focus | Visual Emphasis |
|---------|-------|-----------------|
| **Mucha** | Poster art, elaborate borders | Byzantine halo elements, decorative frames, figurative art |
| **Klimt** | Gold leaf, geometric within organic | Gold mosaic patterns, geometric fills inside organic shapes |
| **Tiffany** | Stained glass, luminous color | Jewel tones, light radiating through, nature scenes |
| **Gaudi** | Architectural, 3D organic forms | Mosaic textures, organic architecture, structural depth |
| **Morris** | Dense botanical patterns | Interlocking botanical motifs, medieval influence, wallpaper density |

## Visual Elements

- Sinuous, flowing "whiplash" curves inspired by natural forms
- Asymmetrical but balanced compositions
- Lines that flow into and become forms
- Organic shapes derived from plants, flowers, insects — stylized rather than realistic
- Decorative borders and frames integral to composition
- Negative space as active design element
- Smooth gradients suggesting depth within organic forms
- Metallic accents (gold, bronze, copper)
- Stained glass luminosity effect
- Intertwining vines, tendrils, peacock feathers, dragonfly accents

## Typography

- Elegant flowing letterforms integrated with organic curves
- Gold or metallic title treatments
- Text within decorative banner ribbons or cartouches
- Ornamental capitals and drop caps
- No modern sans-serif — use serif or art nouveau display style

## Best For

User journeys, growth narratives, interconnected systems, luxury/premium concepts, lifecycle explanations, ecosystem overviews
